Required Fields for Monster Template

Number of Attacks (Max 4)
To hit bonus per attack (these can be different)
Damage Dice Per Attack (eg 1d4, 1d6, 2d6, etc)
	If you like you can leave this as a damage range
Damage Bonus Per Attack
Crit Range
Critical Multiplier
Sneak Attack Damage Dice (eg 1d6, 2d6, etc)
Bonus Damage Dice (eg, bonus fire damage or something like the bleeding on you axe for instance)
